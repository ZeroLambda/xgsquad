Welcome to XG Squad – Our Family, Our Bond

XG Squad celebrates the heart and soul of our family—Xavier, Gracy, and the squad of four. A journey of love, laughter, and cherished memories.

Xavier – The Rock of Our Family

Once the backbone of a defense factory, crafting war tanks for the nation's safety, Xavier, now in his 70s, embraces retirement. His resilience and dedication are an inspiration to the entire family.

Gracy – The Heart of the Squad

A devoted homemaker in her late 60s, Gracie is the glue that binds the family together. Her boundless energy and love make her a favorite among her grandchildren.

Deepa – The Guiding Light

A dedicated teacher and mother of two energetic boys, Deepa nurtures young minds both at home and in the classroom, spreading knowledge and kindness.

Anto-O-Rio – The Visionary

With a passion for storytelling, Rio is a filmmaker determined to bring his creative vision to life. His perseverance inspires the squad to dream big.

Javier – The Tech Explorer

A tech-savvy professional settled in Canada, Javier connects the family through innovation. As the author of this website, he showcases the squad's unity and love.

Mahima – The Creator

A creative entrepreneur with a passion for homemade chocolates, Mahima runs a thriving business. Her designs and creations bring joy to clients and family alike.
