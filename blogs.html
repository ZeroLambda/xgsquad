Our Elakurichi Trip 2023

The Adaikala Madha Shrine in Elakurichi, Tamil Nadu, is a significant Catholic pilgrimage site dedicated to Our Lady of Refuge (Adaikkala Madha in Tamil Nadu) Established in the early 18th century by the Italian Jesuit missionary Constanzo Beschi, known in Tamil as Veeramamunivar, the church stands as a testament to the region's rich religious and cultural heritage.
